Self Branding

The goal I had for this Self Branding was to be able to show people around me where I draw my inspiration from. I've always been a fan of cursive fonts and just beautiful flowy text and thats what I wanted my brand to embody. The idea behind the designs came from an idea I got thinking about all my travels and who i've met in the design world. I draw upon my experiences to gain inspiration and just like that I came up with the idea to have post cards showcase that idea there are 6 post cards all with different parts of the world to show where i've been and who ive been able to connect with in the design world and see how different cultures bring out different creative styles.
